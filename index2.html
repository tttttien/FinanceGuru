Frequently Asked Questions

How long does each course take?

Each course typically runs for a semester, which is approximately 12-16 weeks, depending on the specific schedule and institution.

What are the condition for enrolling in Mathematics 9?

Students should have completed Advanced Mathematics 8 or an equivalent course and possess a strong understanding of fundamental mathematical concepts.

How can I enroll in a course?

Enrollment can be completed online through our website in Courses section or by visiting our tutoring center in person.

Can I get a refund if I withdraw from a course

Refund policies vary depending on the time of withdrawal. Generally, a full refund is available if you withdraw before the course begins, and partial refunds may be available if you withdraw within the first few weeks. Please refer to our refund policy for detailed information.

Are there any additional materials required for the courses?

Yes, students may need to purchase textbooks and other materials such as calculators for Mathematics, novels for Literature, and writing tools for English. Specific requirements will be provided upon enrollment.

What kind of support if I struggle with the course?

We offer a variety of support options including one-on-one tutoring sessions, study groups, and office hours with instructors to ensure you receive the help you need.
